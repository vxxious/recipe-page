Classic Spaghetti Bolognese

This hearty Italian pasta dish combines a rich tomato and meat sauce with perfectly cooked spaghetti. A comforting meal that’s perfect for family dinners or special occasions.

Recipe Information

Prep time: 15 minutes

Cook time: 1 hour

Servings: 4

Difficulty: Beginner

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 500g ground beef
  • 2 cans (400g each) chopped tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 carrot, grated
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • 400g spaghetti
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Heat olive oil in a large pan, add onion and garlic, and sauté until fragrant.
  2. Add ground beef, cook until browned, breaking it up with a spoon.
  3. Stir in tomatoes, tomato paste, carrot, oregano, and basil. Season with salt and pepper.
  4. Reduce heat, cover, and let the sauce simmer for 45 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  5. Meanwhile, cook spaghetti according to package instructions until al dente.
  6. Serve the sauce over spaghetti, garnish with parsley, and enjoy!

Tips

For a richer flavor, let the sauce simmer longer. You can also add a splash of red wine during cooking for extra depth.
